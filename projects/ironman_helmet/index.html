Iron Man MK3 Helmet

A complete build guide for your own wearable Iron Man helmet

Getting Started

Welcome to the Iron Man MK3 Helmet build guide. This project will walk you through creating your own wearable Iron Man helmet using 3D printing, basic electronics, and some patience. The end result is a fully functional helmet with motorized faceplate and LED effects.

Download STL Files

Required Components

3D Printing

  • PLA or PETG Filament (1kg)
  • 3D Printer (220x220mm minimum)
  • Support Material

Electronics

  • Arduino Nano
  • 2x Servo Motors
  • LED Strips (for eyes)
  • 9V Battery + Connector
  • Jumper Wires
  • 2x Toggle Switches

Assembly

  • Super Glue / CA Glue
  • Elastic Head Strap
  • M3 Screws and Nuts
  • Sandpaper (120-400 grit)
  • Primer Spray
  • Metallic Red & Gold Paint
  • Clear Coat
